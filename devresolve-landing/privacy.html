Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

DevResolve, LLC ("DevResolve", "we", "us", or "our") operates the DevResolve platform, including the chat widget, API, and dashboard. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, company name, and billing information processed via Stripe.

Usage data: We log API requests, chat sessions, escalation events, and error rates to operate and improve the service. Chat session content (questions and answers) is used to generate support tickets and docs gap reports.

Knowledge base content: When you connect a knowledge source (Confluence, Notion, GitHub, etc.), we fetch and embed document content into a vector database to power search. We do not store the raw document content permanently — only the vector embeddings.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the DevResolve service
  • To create support tickets and escalation notifications on your behalf
  • To generate docs gap reports for your team
  • To send transactional emails (escalation alerts, onboarding)
  • To detect and prevent abuse

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic / OpenAI — LLM inference for answer synthesis (queries only, no persistent storage on their side)
  • Pinecone — vector search (embeddings only, no raw text)
  • SendGrid — transactional email delivery
  • Your configured ticketing platform (Zendesk, Jira, or Linear) — ticket content

4. Data Retention

Chat session history is retained in-memory for 30 minutes, then discarded. Docs gap logs are retained for 90 days. Vector embeddings are retained until you delete your account or remove the source.

5. Sovereign Plan

Sovereign plan customers run DevResolve entirely within their own infrastructure. No data passes through DevResolve servers. Zero data retention on our side.

6. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. We use fail2ban, UFW firewall, SSH key-only access, and non-root deployment on our production infrastructure. We conduct regular security audits.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing devresolve.ai@outlook.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email devresolve.ai@outlook.com.
