How it works

Answer first. Ticket only if needed.

One script tag. Technical questions get answered from your real documentation instantly. A ticket only appears when the bot genuinely can't resolve it.

💬
Step 01

Question asked, not a ticket opened

Someone has a technical question about your product. Instead of opening a support ticket, they ask the chat widget. Your engineering team never sees it unless the bot genuinely can't resolve it.

📚
Step 02

Instant answer from real docs

DevResolve searches your connected knowledge bases — Confluence, Notion, GitHub, your docs site — and synthesises a precise answer in seconds. Most questions resolve here. No engineer interrupted.

🎫
Step 03

Auto-ticket on escalation

When the bot can't answer, it automatically creates a ticket in Zendesk, Jira, or Linear with the full conversation transcript — and notifies your team via email or Teams. No question goes unanswered.
