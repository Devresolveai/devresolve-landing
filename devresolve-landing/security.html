Anti-Scraping Policy

Anti-Scraping & AI Training Ban

Accessing this site to crawl data for the purpose of training machine learning models or extracting competitive intelligence is a direct violation of our terms. We reserve the right to pursue legal action for unauthorized data harvesting.

This site and all content hosted at devresolve.ai is explicitly excluded from use in:

  • Training, fine-tuning, or pre-training of any large language model (LLM), diffusion model, or AI system.
  • Automated competitive intelligence gathering or market analysis tools.
  • Dataset construction, web archives, or AI benchmark corpora.
  • Any commercial product that incorporates or derives value from DevResolve's proprietary content without explicit written consent.

Automated access to this site is governed by our robots.txt file. Non-compliance with those directives, combined with commercial intent, may constitute unauthorized computer access under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) and equivalent laws.

If you believe you have a legitimate research or journalistic use case, contact us at legal@devresolve.ai before proceeding.

Data Policy

Sovereign Data Policy (BYOK)

DevResolve operates on a "Sovereign" architecture. Enterprise clients utilize their own LLM API keys (Bring Your Own Key), ensuring that sensitive data never leaves their controlled environment.

For Sovereign plan clients, the following guarantees apply:

  • Zero data transit through DevResolve infrastructure — your incident data, team conversations, and resolution outputs are processed entirely within your own cloud or on-premises environment.
  • No AI training on your data — DevResolve never uses client data to train, fine-tune, or improve its models.
  • LLM API costs remain yours — you connect your own provider keys. DevResolve does not intermediate or observe your model interactions.
  • Audit-ready architecture — full logging and RBAC controls are available to support SOC 2, ISO 27001, and internal compliance reviews.

Standard and Professional plan clients benefit from DevResolve's secure multi-tenant infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell, share, or monetize client data under any circumstances.
